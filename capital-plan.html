UK Cascade Revenue — The Immediate Multiplier

Before going international, there's an immediate revenue multiplier sitting in the UK market. One client = multiple benefit applications = multiple fees, all from the same intake interview.

Client applies for Attendance Allowance → fee: £1,073
  ↳ AA award unlocks Pension Credit eligibility → fee: £936
    ↳ PC award unlocks Council Tax Reduction → fee: £270
      ↳ PC triggers Winter Fuel Payment (automatic, no fee)
  ↳ If they have a carer → Carer's Allowance → fee: £809

ONE PENSIONER = UP TO £3,088 IN FEES
Same client. Same intake interview. Zero additional CAC.

The cascade turns every AA client from a £1,073 transaction into a potential £3,088 transaction — a 2.9× revenue multiplier with zero additional acquisition cost.
